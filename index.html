Artist Statement

“The whole of anything is greater than its parts”. For me, the parts are just as important as the whole. This is why in every picture I take, I put just as much attention to the intention as the environment I set up. If you see my new initiative; a new way of seeing Ethiopia, I wish to change the narrative that has been poorly written about the lands that are aesthetically rich in nature and community. As there are virtues that need to be magnified, heroes that ought to be glorified, truths that should be seen.”

I believe understanding the subject in depth remains to be the key to capturing the life and soul of a moment more than just capturing a scenery.
